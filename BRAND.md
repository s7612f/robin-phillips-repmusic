## Typography (Website Style)

- **Headings:** Elegant serif or art-deco-leaning display font (period jazz aesthetic)
- **Body:** Clean, readable sans-serif
- **Tone of type:** Sophisticated, warm — not flashy. Think supper-club menu, not concert poster.

For social assets, favour:
- Uppercase or small-caps for short labels/tags
- Script or handwritten accents for personality
- High contrast text on dark photography

## Brand Voice & Tone

**Personality:** Sophisticated, warm, witty, passionate. Knows its music inside out. Never pompous.

**Speaks to:** Event planners, wedding couples, jazz fans, venue bookers, festival organisers, music lovers aged 30–65.

**Tone on social:** Conversational but polished. Behind-the-scenes warmth. Celebrates the musicians as much as the music.

**Avoid:** Generic "hire us for your event" energy. Corporate stiffness. Over-edited, over-filtered imagery.

### YouTube Thumbnail

> Robin Phillips is a London & Cambridge-based singer, pianist, bandleader and producer. As the founder of repmusic, he delivers the highest quality jazz, swing and pop — live, recorded, and broadcast. His debut originals album *Return From The Source* is out now on all streaming platforms.

### Press / EPK Bio (Long — 150 words)
> Robin Phillips is a singer, pianist, composer, arranger, bandleader, radio presenter and educator based in London and Cambridge. As founder of repmusic Ltd, he has spent decades at the top of the UK jazz and swing scene — leading the acclaimed Pinstripe Suit speakeasy residency for over ten years and hosting more than 100 hours of broadcast radio on *The Jazz Show With Robin Phillips*.
>
> His debut album of original compositions, *Return From The Source*, draws on a 2019 Harley Davidson pilgrimage through the Mississippi Delta and New Orleans — a journey captured in the award-winning documentary *Back To The Source*. Composed during the COVID lockdown, the album features an extraordinary cast including drummer Billy Kilson, trumpeter Leroy Jones, the Soul Sanctuary Gospel Choir, and a string quartet.
>
