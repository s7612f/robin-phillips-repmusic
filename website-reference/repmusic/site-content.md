## About repmusic

repmusic is the online home of Robin Phillips: **singer, pianist, composer, arranger, bandleader, and teacher**.

Beyond Robin's solo work, repmusic arranges:
- Live performances from **Pinstripe Suit** swing band
- A collection of **top female vocalists** for events

Additional services added over time:
- Boutique **recording studio** (Cambridge, est. 2015)
- **Commercial music** — film, library, apps
- **Teaching** — voice, piano, jazz harmony, music tech
- **Piano shell hire** — baby grand pianos
- **TV & Radio presenting**

Website sections: news, gig listings, photo galleries, videos, EPK downloads, audio samples, social media, contact.

## Recording Studio

- **Location:** Cambridgeshire village, near Cambridge
- **Established:** 2015
- **Featured in:** Sound On Sound magazine
- **Engineer:** Robin Phillips (professional session singer/pianist)

### Equipment
**Keyboards:** 1970s Yamaha U3 piano · 1980s Rhodes electric piano · Nord Piano4 · Electro 5D

**Microphones:** Neumann U87 · Neumann U67 · Neumann 47fet · Schoeps · Coles 4038 · Pearlman TM47 · AEA R84 + others

**Preamps:** Neve · API · UA · DAV

### Services
- Vocals & piano recording (speciality)
- Live accompaniment or programmed backing tracks
- Video recording (Sony ZV1 + GoPro, multicam editing)
- Location recording
- Minimal room sound aesthetic

### Ideal clients
Singer-songwriters · Vocalists · Brass/strings/guitar instrumentalists · Demo and album projects
