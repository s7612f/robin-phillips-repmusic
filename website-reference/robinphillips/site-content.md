### The Backstory

**2019:** Robin takes a Harley Davidson road trip through Mississippi Delta and New Orleans.

**6 months later:** Returns to film the award-winning documentary *Back To The Source*. While staying in a New Orleans hotel, unable to sleep listening to the St Charles Streetcar, he begins writing songs in stream-of-consciousness format, continuing on an all-night flight back to England.

**COVID lockdown:** Career stalled. Designates Sundays as composing days. Composes 10 tracks drawing inspiration from:
- New Orleans
- Muscle Shoals Sound Studio
- Sam Phillips (Sun Studio founder)
- His family
- Clarksdale, Mississippi
- The book *South Of Haunted Dreams* by Eddie L. Harris
- Meditation
- The global jazz scene
- Metaphysics
- England

## About Robin Phillips

Robin Phillips is a singer, pianist, bandleader, presenter, producer, and educator.

In 2006 he left a PR post-graduate position to build a full-time music career in London and Cambridge.

### Current Roles
- **Pinstripe Suit** — arranger and bandleader of the speakeasy swing band (10+ year Nightjar residency, London)
- **Documentary presenter/producer** — *Back To The Source*
- **Radio presenter/producer** — *The Jazz Show With Robin Phillips* (100+ hours, Delux Radio, during lockdown)
- **Music educator** — voice, piano, jazz harmony, music tech, band-craft
- **Artist producer** — home recording studio near Cambridge
- **Session musician/composer** — extensive work with jazz-house phenomenon Berlioz
