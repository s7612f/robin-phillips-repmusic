## General Notes for Claude Design

- Always use real performance photography where possible — avoid stock photo aesthetics
- Gold is the accent: use it sparingly for maximum impact (headlines, borders, rules)
- Navy is the foundation: backgrounds, large blocks
- Cream/off-white is the text colour on dark backgrounds
- Never use bright white — keep it warm and slightly aged
- Font pairing to aim for: a period-feel serif (like Playfair Display, Cormorant, or Bodoni) with a clean grotesque (like Helvetica Neue or Aktiv Grotesk)
- Every asset should feel like it belongs in a jazz venue, not a Canva template
